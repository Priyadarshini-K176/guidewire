def calculate_weekly_premium(worker_profile, zone_data, forecast_data):

## 🚨 Adversarial Defense & Anti-Spoofing Strategy

> **Threat detected — March 20, 2026.**
> A coordinated syndicate of 500+ delivery workers, organized via Telegram, is using GPS-spoofing apps to fake locations inside red-alert weather zones — draining liquidity pools with mass simultaneous false claims. Simple GPS verification is officially obsolete. Here's how GigShield fights back.

---

### 1. Differentiation — Genuine Stranded Worker vs. Bad Actor

The core insight: **a real stranded worker leaves a fundamentally different digital footprint than someone spoofing from their couch.** Our AI cross-correlates 7 independent signal layers simultaneously. A spoofer can fake GPS. They cannot fake all 7 at once.

```
THE GIGSHIELD TRUST SCORE ENGINE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  SIGNAL LAYER          GENUINE WORKER           GPS SPOOFER
  ─────────────         ──────────────           ──────────────
  📍 GPS Coordinates    In rain zone             In rain zone ✓ (faked)
  📶 Network Cell ID    Tower near rain zone     Home tower   ✗ MISMATCH
  🌡️ Device Sensors     Humidity spike detected  Stable indoor humidity ✗
  📱 Screen behavior    Checking shelter/maps    Netflix / idle pattern ✗
  🚴 Accelerometer      Stationary (sheltering)  Stationary (lying down) ~
  🛵 Platform API       Last order 12 min ago    Last order 3 days ago  ✗
  🕐 Historical Pattern Same zone all week       Teleported 8km in 2min ✗

  TRUST SCORE: [0.0 ──────────────|────────────── 1.0]
                FRAUD            0.35           GENUINE
```

The syndicate's fatal flaw: GPS apps spoof coordinates — they do not spoof cell tower IDs, barometric pressure readings, or the absence of recent delivery activity. Our model requires corroborating signals across every layer, not just a pin on a map.

---

### 2. The Data — Beyond GPS Coordinates

#### Layer A — Device Telemetry (On-Device SDK)

| Signal | What It Proves | Spoofability |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| **Cell Tower ID + Signal Strength** | Physical proximity via carrier triangulation — independent of GPS | Requires SIM-level hardware hack — extremely rare |
| **Barometric Pressure Sensor** | Drops measurably during heavy rain events | Cannot be software-spoofed without rooted device |
| **Accelerometer / Gyroscope Pattern** | Sheltering rider micro-motion ≠ person lying in bed | Computationally expensive to fake continuously |
| **Ambient Light Sensor** | Outdoors vs. indoors differ significantly — especially daytime rain | Rarely considered by spoofers; high signal value |
| **Battery Drain Rate** | Navigation + weather apps drain faster than a spoofing script in background | Used as a soft corroborating signal |

#### Layer B — Behavioral & Platform Intelligence

| Signal | What It Proves | Anti-Syndicate Value |
|--------|---------------|----------------------|
| **Last Confirmed Order Timestamp** | Genuine worker was active on the platform before the disruption | Syndicate members often haven't delivered in days |
| **30-Day Delivery Zone History** | Workers operate in consistent geographic corridors | Appearing in a flood zone they've never worked in = red flag |
| **Order Acceptance Rate Drop** | Legitimate disruption causes platform-wide acceptance drop — individual vs. cohort comparison | Spoofers show no change in app interaction patterns |
| **In-App Session Behavior** | Checking weather/shelter apps = genuine; gaming/streaming = spoofing indicator | Session metadata only — no content accessed |

#### Layer C — Coordinated Ring Detection (The Syndicate Killer)

```
COORDINATED FRAUD RING DETECTOR
════════════════════════════════

  Step 1: SPATIAL CLUSTERING ALERT
  ─────────────────────────────────
  IF (claims from same geo-cell spike > 3x baseline within 15 min)
  → Flag entire cluster for elevated scrutiny
  → Real rain event  = gradual organic claim ramp-up
  → Syndicate attack = sudden coordinated spike  ← THIS IS THE TELL

  Step 2: SOCIAL GRAPH ANALYSIS
  ──────────────────────────────
  IF (flagged claimants share device IDs, referral chains, or
      phone number prefixes from onboarding data)
  → Compute syndicate_affinity_score
  → Shared referrer + simultaneous claims = high fraud signal

  Step 3: TEMPORAL PATTERN MATCHING
  ───────────────────────────────────
  IF (claim timestamps cluster within seconds across multiple accounts)
  → Indicates automated bot/script — not organic human behavior
  → Genuine workers file claims at naturally distributed intervals

  Step 4: VELOCITY GATING
  ────────────────────────
  One UPI ID = one policy. Enforced hard at KYC onboarding.
  Prevents one person operating multiple fake accounts.
```

### 3. UX Balance — Protecting Honest Workers from False Positives

> The hardest problem in fraud detection isn't catching fraudsters — it's not punishing honest people. A rider genuinely stranded in a flash flood must not have their payout delayed because our model is over-tuned.

```
CLAIM RESOLUTION TIERS
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  TIER 1 — AUTO-APPROVE  (Trust Score < 0.35)
  ────────────────────────────────────────────
  All signals corroborate. Instant payout. Zero friction.
  Worker sees: "₹450 credited — GigShield protected you today 🛡️"
  SLA: < 60 seconds

  TIER 2 — SOFT-HOLD + SELF-CURE  (Trust Score 0.35–0.65)
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Ambiguous signals (e.g., cell tower lag during network congestion
  in heavy rain — this genuinely happens). Worker is NOT accused.

  → App message: "We're verifying your location due to network
    signals. Tap to confirm with one quick photo."
  → One-tap photo: timestamp + background metadata checked
  → Photo matches location → INSTANT APPROVE ✅
  → Genuine network dead zone → 2-hour grace window,
    auto-approved when connectivity restores

  SLA: < 2 hours worst case. No fraud accusation in language.

  TIER 3 — HARD FLAG + HUMAN REVIEW  (Trust Score > 0.65)
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Multiple strong contradictory signals + cluster spike detected.

  → Worker notified: "Your claim is under review. We'll respond
    by [time]." — neutral language, no accusation.
  → Human / LLM-assisted triage within 4 hours
  → Cleared? Full payout + ₹25 goodwill credit for the wait
  → Confirmed fraud? Account suspended, flagged to platform.

  SLA: < 4 hours.
```

**Our fairness commitments — non-negotiable:**

- ✅ We never auto-reject. Claims either auto-approve or escalate.
- ✅ The word "fraud" never appears in worker-facing communication.
- ✅ Genuine network drops in bad weather → grace window, not rejection.
- ✅ Every false-positive cleared worker gets ₹25 goodwill credit automatically.
- ✅ All Tier 3 decisions are logged and auditable for systematic bias review.
- ✅ Workers can appeal any decision via one tap — reviewed within 24 hours.

**Why this collapses the syndicate attack specifically:** The Telegram syndicate's power is coordination speed — they trigger simultaneously and overwhelm manual review. Our spatial clustering detector catches the attack signature within 15 minutes. Individual spoofers are caught by multi-layer device telemetry. And the Tier 2 photo check — asking someone to show they're physically outside in rain — is something no one at home can fake. Each check can theoretically be beaten alone. All of them together, for 500 accounts simultaneously, is not a realistic attack surface.

> **The syndicate can spoof GPS. They cannot spoof being outside in a rainstorm.**
